About

Everyday Life: International Journal of Public Culture, Art and Design is a newly established independent open-access journal dedicated to public culture, urban space, art, design, and the politics of everyday life.

The journal welcomes interdisciplinary approaches to everyday environments, cultural production, spatial practice, visual culture, media, infrastructure, public art, design research, and lived experience.
